A BIT ABOUT ME

The first time I wrote code was when I was 9 years old on a Commodore 64C. I was impressed that I could make the computer do something that I wanted it to do. Since then, I never stopped writing code. Usually I am working on a side project. My skills revolve around web development and mobile app development. I also love contributing to the open source software. I like skiing, biking, and urban exploring. But sometimes it's best to relax with a movie or a good TV show, with lemonade in front of me. When I have a lot of free time, I like to explore the world! I have visited numerous countries, lived in several, encountered many cultures, and seen amazing sights. I am a Pluralsight author focusing on the Python programming language. I am very proud to say that my Python: Getting Started course is the 5th most-popular course on Pluralsight!
